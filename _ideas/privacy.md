# Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026

Clipper accesses your TikTok account solely to post videos on your behalf.
We do not store, sell, or share your personal data or TikTok credentials
with third parties. Access tokens are stored securely and used only for
posting. You can revoke access at any time via TikTok's app settings.
