## What This Certification Covers

The program focuses on the fundamentals required to build and manage products effectively, including:

- product vision and strategy,
- user-centered problem definition,
- prioritization and roadmap thinking,
- stakeholder alignment,
- collaboration with engineering and design teams.

Rather than tooling or frameworks alone, the emphasis is on **decision-making**, **trade-offs**, and **product thinking**.
