---

## Google Cloud Professional Cloud Architect

The certification focuses on designing and operating cloud-native systems capable of supporting real-world business and technical requirements.

Key areas covered include:
- designing scalable and resilient cloud architectures,
- networking and hybrid connectivity,
- security, IAM, and compliance,
- infrastructure reliability and disaster recovery,
- Kubernetes and containerized workloads,
- cost optimization and operational excellence,
- data and AI platform integration on GCP.

The program places strong emphasis on making architecture decisions that balance:
- scalability,
- security,
- reliability,
- maintainability,
- and business constraints.
